About the Studio

From various and distinct areas, it being Architecture, Arts, Music, and Philosophy, each member of our Team brings their input to the table in order to make our Projects more complete. We believe in Architecture as a symbol of progress, critique and innovation. From the Materials and Matter, Space and Void, Urbanism and Society, we focus on delievering an Architecture object for any and everyone. We hope to leave our marks of hope in this World and build a better future.

Botânico

Botânico's House was our first project as a team. An Architectural Object comissioned by a dear botanist friend. Integrating Nature and Construction, we aimed for a symbiosis between the interior space and the outside, integrating this Object inside the terrain.
